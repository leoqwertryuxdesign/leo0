Remini

Volunteering: The force of citizens helping the city of Detroit to improve and become a safer place

Client: George Brown College
Role: App Design, UX/UI, Design Strategy & Branding
Tools Used: InVision, Photoshop, Illustrator & After Effects
Duration: 4 months



OVERVIEW


Detroiteer is a mobile application for volunteers in Detroit. The app allows people to find and participate in a diverse range of activities with the objective of revitalizing their city.

After completing each task, users are rewarded with Detroit Coins: a cryptocurrency controlled and generated by the city council. Volunteers are able to use their earned coins in participant stores and buy products that suit their needs.

Detroiteer is aimed for unemployed & low-income citizens or anyone that wants to transform Detroit into a cleaner and safer environment.




The result of volunteer work rewards people with virtual money made possible through cryptocurrency

Detroiteer App

The following information is about the design process of the Detroiteer app and its cryptocurrency, including each step from concept to the final design and challenges that were faced during the development.


RESEARCH


This project was developed with the objective of proposing a tool to improve the life quality of the citizens in Detroit. For the initial phase, a research was conducted to find the key problem faced by the city and analyze the possibilities we have based on their current situation.

The research revealed a large number of problems:

- Poverty rates
- High occurrence of crime
- Poor infrastructure
- Population decline
- Abandoned buildings

All the information we gathered served as the main channel of inspiration for our proposed solutions. The following step was to summarize what we learned into personas and generate a visualization of a typical citizen of Detroit.


The Problem: Violence, poverty, population decline and a lack of infrastructure across the city



CONCEPT & DESIGN


After analyzing what we learned from the research, we began a brainstorming phase to generate concepts that could be applied in the city.

The initial sketches of the selected project were created: a mobile application that allows citizens to help their community through volunteer work.

Initial sketches of Detroiteer


The next step was the creation of high-fidelity wireframes. Iterations were made in order to achieve an efficient interface design and guarantee satisfaction by our users. The UI follows Google's Material Design standards.

It was time to select a name and the decision was made after a combination of the words Detroit + Volunteer: Detroiteer.


We began to create the visual identity and all the necessary assets of Detroiteer, including the implementation of the cryptocurrency system. The goal was to have an interactive prototype that people can test it and understand the functionalities of the application.

The logo, colour palette & typography were defined in order to have a consistent brand:

With the definition of Detroiteer's branding, I designed mockups for all screens of the mobile application, following the project's style guide and the Material Design elements (used for icons & buttons).

In order to have a cryptocurrency for this project, Ethereum Wallet was used to generate a token and simulate the process of mining Detroit Coins.

Detroit Coin

RESULT


The mockups were approved and finalized, the specifications of the cryptocurrency coin were defined and the next step was to build a prototype for the application using InVision. In addition, the instructions for using Detroiteer were clear:

- Create an account and log in

- Search for volunteer opportunities around your location

- Apply for an activity

- Attend the event and contribute to the revitalization of Detroit

- Get rewarded with Detroit Coins

- Use coins at selected stores and services

Detroit is currently facing a large number of problems that have to be minimized in order to guarantee a better quality of life for its citizens. Unfortunately, ending the situation of this city requires many years. This application aims to assist individuals that are struggling financially and to revitalize the parks, streets, neighbourhoods & abandoned areas that lack minimum care.


- Final considerations:
Overall I had a great time during the development of Detroiteer. There were some challenges with the cryptocurrency implementation but Ethereum Wallet proportionated us with a visualization of the coins. This project met my expectations and it became a concept that I would like to continue working on and develop the system.
