Who Wants 1

Who Wants 1

An app concept for ordering services within your community

Client: George Brown College
Role: UX/UI & App Design
Tools Used: Photoshop, Illustrator & Balsamiq
Duration: 2 months



OVERVIEW


Who Wants 1 is a mobile application that allows you to order a variety of services provided by your community at a few taps of your phone.

After creating an account, users are able to find posts made by other people near them and order the items & services that are being offered. The application includes services such as:

- Catering
- Transportation
- Shopping
- Elderly Assistance
- Cleaning & many others

The application connects individuals based on their social media contacts but also allows users to order services from people that are not part of their friends' list.




Who Wants 1 provides a large variety of services and connects individuals based on their necessities and location

Who Wants 1 App

The information below is about the design process of Who Wants 1 and the system behind it. It includes each step from concept to the final design and all challenges that were faced during the development of this project.


RESEARCH


The initial phase was to research people's daily routine and check for areas that could be improved in order to facilitate and provide a better quality of life.

The gathered information revealed that a large number of individuals complain about time. Many people mentioned that they are always busy and that they desire to spend more time with their families and friends.

The idea of time became the main focus of our brainstorming phase. How to make people feel less busy with their daily tasks? How to provide them with opportunities to have more free time? After many concepts, we agreed that for most of these individuals getting help from other people would make their lives a bit easier. CONCEPT & DESIGN


The concept was defined: a mobile application that allows users to find the services that they need. The app would be focused on connecting people based on their contact list and display posts from friends, neighbours and individuals that the user feels safe to interact with.

With the UX approval, the next step was to create wireframes with Balsamiq and visualize the functionalities and layout of the application.


During this phase, the app got a name: Who Wants 1. In addition, we began to create the visual identity and assets of the app such as icons and images.

Our concept was clear at this point: users can post their services and look for other people's offers. The categories were selected, the project was approved and it was time to produce the mockups.


RESULT


The project was finalized and we met the goal of having a concept, wireframes and mockups that visualizes Who Wants 1's system.


- Final considerations:
Who Wants 1 met our expectations and during the duration of the development (1 month) I learned about the importance of having a schedule and following a plan in order to guarantee efficiency and quality of work. Since it was a short period of time to commit to each phase of the project, we faced some challenges but they were overcome with the implementation of time management methods in the design process.
