FestivAR

FestivAR

Augmented Reality: A tool to enhance our experience in festivals & events


Client: George Brown College
Role: UX, Branding & AR Development
Tools Used: Unity, Vuforia, Photoshop, Illustrator & After Effects
Duration: 2 months



OVERVIEW


FestivAR is a service for festival organizers with the goal of implementing augmented reality into their business and generate unique experiences for the attendees.

It also includes an application that allows users to find and interact with the content through their smartphones, tablets or smartglasses. The service combines the usage of mobile devices and the latest AR technologies to immerse & assist people inside festivals.





Finding new ways to implement AR in the entertainment industry

Night music festival with FestivAR

The following information is about the design & development process of the FestivAR concept and a prototype of the Augmented Reality technology.


RESEARCH


The initial phase was to conduct a research and look for new ways and fields that Augmented Reality (AR) can be implemented. This technology is rapidly growing in popularity and becoming more common for users due to the game industry. Games like Pokémon GO demonstrated how AR can be used as an engaging way to bring content that would be impossible in any other way.

Augmented Reality allows us to create a new world of interactivity and unique tools to assist us in our daily lives. In addition, it can be applied to a large number of fields such as education, healthcare, transportation, manufacturing and others.

From the information that we gathered, the decision was to focus on the entertainment field and find opportunities to apply Augmented Reality.



CONCEPT


The brainstorming phase began and based on the research findings some concepts were created. After carefully analyzing them and visualizing the possibilities, the selected one was about the creation of a service for festival organizers and an application that allows people to see AR content in the events.




Implementing Augmented Reality beyond the game industry


With the definition of our concept, the project got a name: FestivAR. The service would be provided by a third party company and the AR content created based on the desires of the organizer and the festival's theme. For example, let's imagine a festival about dinosaurs.

Currently, this type of festival relies on physical models or costumes in order to decorate their space. However, with Augmented Reality the possibilities become endless and dinosaurs can come back to life through mobile device screens. This system is also much affordable, efficient and faster than the construction of physical creatures. In addition, the content can be updated or modified at any moment by the organizers.

Another advantage of AR props & decorations over the standard ones is the fact that it generates a more immersive experience for the audience and new ways for companies to place their advertisements. Many categories of events would benefit from the application of Augmented Reality. For this project, we selected dinosaur festivals to showcase the possibilities of AR.

AR Technology


At this point, we began the development process of the Augmented Reality content. In order to bring dinosaurs back to life, it was necessary to understand the AR technology and study about the possibilities & limitations of it. The decision was to use Unity & Vuforia which are powerful tools for Augmented Reality implementation.

Bringing dinosaurs back to life through AR


We began to implement the necessary assets into the Unity scene and test the initial functionalities of the application. The objective was to have a moving dinosaur that users can look at by using FestivAR. Some iterations were required to guarantee the the 3D model appears in the correct size when observing through the mobile screens.

Testing AR creature


In addition to the 3D model, another segment of the application was about virtual markers that allow attendees to check the distance between them and specific locations of the festival (food, attractions and other services).
