FestivAR

FestivAR

Augmented Reality: A tool to enhance our experience in festivals & events

Client: George Brown College
Role: UX, Branding & Augmented Reality Development
Tools Used: Unity, Photoshop, Illustrator & Balsamiq
Duration: 2 months



OVERVIEW


FestivAR is a service for festival organizers with the goal of implementing augmented reality into their business and generate unique experiences for the attendees. It also includes an application that allows users to find and interact with the content through their smartphone or tablet. The service combines the usage of mobile devices and the latest AR technologies in order to immerse & assist people inside festivals.




The result of volunteer work rewards people with virtual money made possible through cryptocurrency

FestivAR App

The following information is about the design process of the FestivAR app and its cryptocurrency, including each step from concept to the final design and challenges that were faced during the development.


RESEARCH


This project was developed with the objective of proposing a tool to improve the life quality of the citizens of Detroit. For the initial phase, a research was conducted to find the key problem faced by the city and analyze the possibilities we have with the current situation of Detroit.

The research revealed a large number of problems:

- Poverty rates
- High occurrence of crime
- Poor infrastructure
- Population decline
- Abandoned buildings

All the information we gathered served as the main channel of inspiration for our proposed solutions. The following step was to summarize what we learned into personas and generate a visualization of a typical citizen of Detroit.


The Problem: Violence, poverty, population decline and a lack of infrastructure across the city



CONCEPT & DESIGN


After analyzing what we learned from the research, we began a brainstorming phase to generate concepts that could be applied in the city of Detroit.

The initial sketches of the selected project were created: a mobile application that allows citizens to help their community through volunteer work.

Initial sketches of FestivAR


The next step was the creation of high-fidelity wireframes. Iterations were made in order to achieve an efficient interface design and guarantee satisfaction by our users. The UI follows Google's Material Design standards.

It was time to select a name and the decision was made after a combination of the words Detroit + Volunteer: FestivAR.


We began to create the visual identity and all the necessary assets of FestivAR, including the implementation of the cryptocurrency system. The goal was to have an interactive prototype that users can test it and understand the functionalities of the application.

The logo, colour palette & typography were defined in order to have a consistent brand:

With the definition of FestivAR's branding, I designed mockups for all screens of the mobile application, following the project's style guide and the Material Design guidelines (used for icons & buttons).

In order to have a cryptocurrency for this project, Ethereum Wallet was used to generate a token and simulate the process of mining Detroit Coins.

Detroit Coin

RESULT


The mockups were approved and finalized, the specifications of the cryptocurrency coin were defined and the next step was to build a prototype for the application using InVision. In addition, the steps for using FestivAR were clear:

- Create an account and log in
- Search for volunteer opportunities around your location
- Apply for an activity
- Attend the event and contribute to the revitalization of Detroit
- Get rewarded with Detroit Coins
- Use coins at selected stores and services

Detroit is currently facing a large number of problems that need to be minimized in order to provide a better quality of life for its citizens. Unfortunately, ending the situation of this city requires many years. This application aims to assist individuals that are struggling financially and to transform the parks, streets, neighbourhoods & abandoned areas that lack minimum care.


- Final considerations:
Overall I had a great time during the development of FestivAR. There were some challenges with the cryptocurrency implementation but Ethereum Wallet proportionated a visualization of the created currency. This project met my expectations and it became a concept that I would like to continue working on and develop its system.
