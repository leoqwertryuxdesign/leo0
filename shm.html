SHM

Designing and developing the website of an architectural firm


Client: SHM Design + Arquitetura
Role: UX/UI, Web Design & Development
Tools Used: Sketch, Balsamiq, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Duration: 2 weeks




CLIENT


SHM Design + Arquitetura is an architectural firm based in Sao Paulo performing design in the commercial, corporate and residential sectors. The company is expanding and requires an online presence in order to showcase their work and attract potential new projects. My responsibilities with this client were to design & develop their website and improve the visual elements of SHM.


Website Visualization

DESIGN PROCESS


The initial stage of this project was to communicate with the client and determine the goals and purpose of their website. During this phase, I got the necessary details about what they desire, who is their target audience, the elements that they wish to display and a general understanding of their services.

Based on the gathered information I started sketching a layout of the page and the user flow in order to visualize the initial design. The client gave me examples of sites that they like and features to include. After analyzing their content I realized that the best approach would be a one page website with an easy access to details about their services, projects and contact information.



At this point, I got feedback from the client based on the one page approach and they approved my layout. In addition, they provided me with some of the necessary assets (titles, description of projects/company & logo). The next stage was to create wireframes and have a more in-depth visualization of the content in the page. My goal was to have a consistent and responsive design that adapts to the device screen.

After presenting the wireframes, I initiated the creation of the visual elements for one of the segments of the page. The client requested simple icons to represent each of their services and I made them with a clean and consistent style in mind following their colours and logo.

During the development of this project, one of my objectives was to always communicate with the client about my progress and new elements that were being added to the website. The next stage was to transform the wireframes into mockups that represent the final product by implementing all the visual assets and design specifications.







Mockups in Sketch

Following the creation of the mockups, I received feedback from the client and made the changes that were requested. After this process, SHM was satisfied with the look and feel of the website and I presented a prototype of it made with InVision.

After the finalization of the mockups and approval of the website design, I started coding the page. Since I had Bootstrap in mind during the initial layout, I implemented this framework to have a more efficient design process.




I managed to finish the coding of the page and all assets were included (descriptions, logo and images that the client sent to me). The firm received all the necessary files and according to their plan, it will be published in early August.




- Final considerations:
I was very satisfied with the work I did with this client. Communicating with the company about my design process at the beginning of the development phase was an important part of the success of this project and I look forward to seeing the published version of their website.
