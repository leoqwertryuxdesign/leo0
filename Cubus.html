Tools Used: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects & Maya
Role: Strategy, UX, UI, Motion Design
Duration: 2 months


OVERVIEW


Cubus (Cube + bus) is a modular & autonomous transit system that allows buses to attach/detach from each other and generate a unique transfer experience.

Each "cube" has a capacity of 16 people and their own set of paths around the city. The modularity permits passengers to move between modules and transfer to the one with their desired destination. With safety and efficiency in mind, Cubus is an efficient way to move round.
