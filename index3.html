WHO AM I?

Hi, I'm Leonardo!
A User Experience and Interface Designer.

As an Interaction Design graduate, I believe that great design is more than just visual beauty or making things simple.

It's the idea of solving problems, accomplish goals and adapting itself to each situation based on the scope of a project, target audience and a diverse range of factors that create challenge and uniquiness to this field. I want to help you reach your goals and solve your user’s problems.

I’m looking forward to the next exciting challenge in order to grow professionally and to bring my design ideas to life.
